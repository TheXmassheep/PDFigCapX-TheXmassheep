4. Performance comparison I: simulation analysis
One advantage of using statistical methods is their ability to separate a systematic pattern of con-
flict events from non-systematic errors. In this section, I compare the performance of both deter-
ministic and statistical methods by conducting a couple of simulation analyses. I first define a
population conflict zone as the entire territory of Nigeria or Somalia. I choose these countries
because they have perhaps the most convex and concave shapes among African countries. I
then randomly draw 1000 locations within the territory and add random noise;
